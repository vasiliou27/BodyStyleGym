## Next: Deploy to Vercel (2 minutes)

1. **Go to:** https://vercel.com
2. **Sign up/Login** with your GitHub account
3. **Click:** "Add New Project" or "Import Project"
4. **Select:** Your `BodyStyleGym` repository
5. **Click:** "Deploy" (all settings are auto-detected)
6. **Wait:** 2-3 minutes for deployment
7. **Get your live URL:** You'll get something like `https://body-style-gym.vercel.app`

## That's it! 

Your website will be live and you can share the URL with anyone!
